Project Brief

Topic Summary:

The theme of this project is the diversity of ethnic festivals. As an important part of national culture, festivals contain characters, mentality, beliefs, ways of thinking and multi-ethnic groups. Different countries and ethnic festivals are also different, they are colorful, distinct, reflecting the local folk customs. Cultural Festival is mainly organized by artists, cultural institutions and local government departments, the state to give some financial support. The rise of modern cultural festival is to revive culture and arouse people's awareness of culture. Today, the world holds thousands of cultural festivals every year. Some carry out a series of activities around an art theme or pass on ethnic beliefs. These festivals provide an important platform for communication between the general public and artists, and help people to establish and share their rich cultural heritage. Therefore, the most important or unique festival of some countries in the east and west is chosen as a festival diversity study. The purpose of the project is to get more people to learn about the diverse cultures of the world through special festivals.

Target Audience:

People interested in the cultural diversity of the world. They can be people of all ages, such as children who are interested in world culture, foreigners living in different countries and teachers who teach world culture. Learn about different world cultures through this project. Showcase the way people live in other countries in the world. Someone who wants to share their national holiday with others. They can upload their pictures of ethnic cultures and help others understand their own holiday.

Project Goals and Workings:

Enhance the exchange of people who love national culture. People living in exotic countries can better accept ethnic diversity and better integrate different cultures. Prioritized on the need to embrace its increasing ethnic diversity and assist diverse populations in overcoming barriers to community participation. If user upload photos to the corresponding festival directory, using Google API analysis of photos to provide the corresponding tag to photos and help other users understand.

Communication Strategy:

Cultural Festival is rich in manifestations, nature and content. Whether it is traditional, modern, urban or rural, it attracts large numbers of people and shares their passion. Cultural Festival is a concentrated expression of world cultural life, giving artists the opportunity to express their views on the world to the public in various ways, which plays an important role in promoting innovation and achieving cultural diversity. Collecting some pictures of holiday-loving artists, increase the communication between these people, and at the same time convey the festival diversity.

Goals for the overall look and feel:

Home page will use simple lines as a starting point, the design will be black. I hope through the simple appearance highlight the color and diversity of festival pictures. Direct online communication will not be much, mainly to enrich the art picture-based, with pictures taken by the photographer's information and communication. If someone is interested in leaving a message or leave a contact, will help further communication between users. Each festival will be accompanied by a text description, a detailed description of the characteristics and origin of this festival. Increase the search function, allowing users to choose their favorite festival culture. Increase the message function, user can leave their own contact information in the message area, so that people who have the same preferences get connections.
